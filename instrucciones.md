### Port Forward

kubectl port-forward <resource_type>/<resource_name> <local_port>:<remote_port>

Forwarding to a Pod:

    kubectl port-forward pod/my-pod 8080:80

Forwarding to a Service:

    kubectl port-forward service/my-service 8080:80
